Pacific Northwest (Oregon to Washington) 2022

Trip with Amy, Kylie and Jack to the PNW! We flew into Oregon and went straight to Olympic National Park. We stayed in Forks and went to Hurricane Ridge! We then took a ferry to Seattle and stay there for a night. We then went north, took a ferry to the San Juan Islands, and camped out there. We then went down to Portland, hung out with John and Emily, then stayed in an AirBnB in the woods. We flew back and missed our connection in Denver, and we spent the night in the airport :(
